About Oracle Academy

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence in technology education

Our Mission

Oracle Academy is Oracle's global, philanthropic education program that advances computing education around the world. We provide schools and students with curriculum, faculty training, software, cloud technology, and support to help students succeed in the global economy.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering database technologies, Java programming, and cloud computing
  • Industry-standard software and development tools
  • Professional certification opportunities
  • Access to Oracle's cloud infrastructure for hands-on learning
  • Career guidance and placement support
